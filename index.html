Biography

I am currently a last-year Master student in School of Software at Shandong University, under the supervision by Prof. Yilong Yin and Haoliang Sun. My research interests include computer vision and machine learning, such as Learning with noisy labels, Semi-supervised Learning, Meta learning, and Partial-label learning.

Thanks to my co-authors Lei Feng (CQU, NTU), Haoliang Sun (SDU).

Feel free to email me if you are interested in talking with me.
