Qi Wei (魏琦)

I am currently a first-year Ph.D. candidate at the College of Computing and Data Science (CCDS), Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, supervised by Prof. Bo An. I also maintain a close collaborative relationship with Prof. Lei Feng from Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD).

My research interests include computer vision and machine learning, such as AI safety and Weakly-supervised Learning (such as Learning with noisy labels and Semi-supervised Learning). Feel free to email me if you are interested in talking with me.
